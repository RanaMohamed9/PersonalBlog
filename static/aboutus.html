Who we are?

Hello! Welcome to bloggery!

  • My name’s Rana and I started this site because I LOVE embroidery.
  • I was born in Egypt.
  • I know how hard and overwhelming it seems to get started and choose an embroidery machine, so I started this site with the intention of making things simple and helping newcomers dip their toes into this wonderful hobby.
  • Me and my team of writers put as much care into our reviews and articles as we do into our embroidery, but if we’ve made a mistake or didn’t answer some important question, please get in touch!
  • We’d love to hear from you – even if it’s a complaint. We want this site to be the best it can be.
  • Happy embroidery!

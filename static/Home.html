What is embroidery And how I start?

Nov 6, 2020

Embroidery is the craft of decorating fabric or other materials using a needle to apply thread or yarn.
Embroidery may also incorporate other materials such as pearls, beads, quills, and sequins. In modern days, embroidery is usually seen on caps, hats, coats, blankets, dress shirts, denim, dresses, stockings, and golf shirts. Embroidery is available with a wide variety of thread or yarn color. Some of the basic techniques or stitches of the earliest embroidery are chain stitch, buttonhole or blanket stitch, running stitch, satin stitch, cross stitch.Those stitches remain the fundamental techniques of hand embroidery today [..]

Embroidery Hoop Shapes

Embroidery hoops are typically found in three shapes:

Circular embroidery hoops are by far the most common.
Oval shaped embroidery hoops are used for wider projects.
Square embroidery hoops are more commonly used for machine embroidery.
